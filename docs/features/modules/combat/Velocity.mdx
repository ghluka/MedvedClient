# Velocity

Modifies knockback you receive from attacks

---

### Mode

The knockback strategy to use.

- **REDUCE**: lower incoming knockback
- **REVERSE**: send knockback back toward the attacker
- **CANCEL**: remove knockback completely
- **JUMP RESET**: use jumps to reset knockback timing
- **DELAY**: delay knockback reactions

### Reduce %

Percent of horizontal knockback to keep in REDUCE mode.

### Reduce y %

Percent of vertical knockback to keep in REDUCE mode.

### Reverse %

Percent of knockback reversed in REVERSE mode.

### Chance %

Chance to perform a jump reset in JUMP RESET mode.

### Timing (ms)

Jump timing window for JUMP RESET mode.

### Ground delay (ms)

Delay before reacting while on ground in DELAY mode.

### Air delay (ms)

Delay before reacting while in air in DELAY mode.
