# Velocity

Modifies knockback you receive from attacks

---

There are multiples modes of Velocity that all work differently, some are ghost
and some are very detected, please read the
[anticheat safety article](/guides/anticheat-safety) for more information.

## Jump Reset <sup><Chip label="Ghost" color="success" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<VerifiedUserIcon />}/></sup>

Jumps after you take damage to legitimately reduce your knockback.

![Example of how the Jump Reset mode affects velocity](./img/velocity-jumpreset.gif)

#### Chance %

How often you jump reset after taking knockback

#### Timing (ms)

How long after you've taken knockback before you jump

## Delay <sup><Chip label="Ghost" color="success" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<VerifiedUserIcon />}/></sup>

Increases your ping so you can still move and attack while you've taken knockback by delaying it,
meaning if someone hits you in a fight you can still hit them while your knockback has been delayed.

![Example of how the Delay mode affects velocity](./img/velocity-delay.gif)

#### Ground delay (ms)

Delay before reacting while on ground

#### Air delay (ms)

Delay before reacting while in air

## Reduce

Attacks the entity you're looking at and scales a specific percent of your knockback.
Works on some predictive anticheats but not all

![Example of how the Reduce mode affects velocity](./img/velocity-reduce.gif)

#### Attack count

How many attacks it will perform on an entity

#### Range

Maximum distance an entity can attacked, this does NOT affect your reach

#### Horizontal

Percent of horizontal knockback to keep (0 = no kb, 1 = full kb)

#### Vertical

Percent of vertical knockback to keep (0 = no kb, 1 = full kb)

## Modify <sup><Chip label="Warning" color="warning" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<WarningIcon />}/></sup>

Scales a specific percent of your knockback

![Example of how the Modify mode affects velocity](./img/velocity-modify.gif)

#### Modify %

Percent of horizontal knockback to remove (0% = full kb, 100% = no kb)

#### Modify y %

Percent of vertical knockback to remove (0% = full kb, 100% = no kb)

## Freeze <sup><Chip label="Warning" color="warning" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<WarningIcon />}/></sup>

Makes the server think your game is frozen, cancelling knockback

![Example of how the Freeze mode affects velocity](./img/velocity-freeze.gif)

## Reverse <sup><Chip label="Warning" color="warning" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<WarningIcon />}/></sup>

After a set amount of ticks (or instant), you will take forwards knockback
by a specified percent of the original knockback
(50% = cancels out original knockback). 

![Example of how Reverse mode affects velocity](./img/velocity-reverse.gif)

## Cancel <sup><Chip label="Warning" color="warning" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<WarningIcon />}/></sup>

Cancels all knockback packets

![Example of how the Cancel mode affects velocity](./img/velocity-cancel.gif)
