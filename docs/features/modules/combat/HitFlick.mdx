# Hit Flick

Silently flicks rotation on each attack to displace knockback sideways

---

### Flick angle

The range of yaw angles used for each flick.

### Flick mode

Direction selection for each hit.

- **LEFT**: flick left every attack
- **RIGHT**: flick right every attack
- **RANDOM**: randomly choose left or right
- **STRAFE**: flick in the direction you are strafing
- **STRAFE INVERTED**: flip the strafe direction

### Require sprint

Only perform flicks while sprinting.

### Cooldown (ms)

Minimum delay between flicks.
