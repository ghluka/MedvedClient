# Hit Swap

Swaps into another weapon on attack, copying its attributes.

Hit Swap gives you automatic weapon switching on the first hit, then returns to your original slot after the attack.

## Weapons

### Maces

- **Maces**: enables swapping to mace items from your hotbar.
- **Smash only**: only swaps to a mace while falling, ensuring a smash-style attack.
- **Require Breach**: only swaps to maces whose item name or description includes `breach`.
- **Require Density**: only swaps to maces whose item name or description includes `density`.
- **Stun Slam**: swaps to an axe first, then follows up with a mace slam on the next attack.

### Spears

- **Spears**: enables swapping to spears from your hotbar on attack.
- **Require Lunge**: only swaps to spears with the lunge enchantment.

### Axes

- **Axes**: enables swapping to axes from your hotbar.
- **Shield only**: only swaps when we're attacking someone blocking with their shield, disabling their shield.

### Swords

- **Swords**: enables swapping to swords from your hotbar.
- **Require Fire Aspect**: only swaps to swords with fire-related enchantments.
