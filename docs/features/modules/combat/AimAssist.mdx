# Aim Assist

Gently nudges your aim toward nearby players without fighting your mouse

---

### Range

The maximum distance in blocks to consider targets.

### FOV

The field of view angle within which aim assist will act.

### Strength

How strongly your aim is nudged toward the target.

### Only when attacking

Only apply assistance while the attack key is held.

### Players only

Limit aim assist to player entities only.
