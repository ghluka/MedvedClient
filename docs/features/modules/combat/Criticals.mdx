# Criticals

Forces critical hits on attacks

---

### Mode

The critical hit strategy to use.

- **NO GROUND**: attempt criticals when you are already off the ground
- **PACKET**: send movement packets to force critical hit detection
- **JUMP**: automatically jump to trigger critical hits

### Packet mode

Packet sequence used in PACKET mode.

### Jump range

The enemy distance threshold used by JUMP mode.
