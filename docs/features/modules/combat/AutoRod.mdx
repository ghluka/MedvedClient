# Auto Rod

Automatically throws your fishing rod, retracts it, or throws projectiles

---

### Retract speed (ms)

How quickly the rod is retracted after a cast.

### Action delay (ms)

Delay before each rod action occurs.

### Randomize delays

Add variation to delay timing for more natural behavior.

### Switch to weapon

Return to your weapon slot after performing the rod action.

### Allow throwables

Enable use of eggs or snowballs instead of the fishing rod.

### Throw speed (ms)

Delay used when throwing projectiles.

### Not within melee range

Disable rod/projectile actions when an enemy is too close.
