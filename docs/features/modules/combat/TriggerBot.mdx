# Trigger Bot <sup><Chip label="Ghost" color="success" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<VerifiedUserIcon />}/></sup>

Automatically attacks when your crosshair is over an enemy

---

### Range

Maximum attack distance.

### Extra delay

Additional ticks to wait after each trigger.

### Players only

Restrict triggers to player entities.

### Only weapon

Fire only when holding a weapon.

### Require mouse down

Only trigger when the attack key is held.

### Shield check

Do not attack targets that are blocking with a shield.

### Air crits

Only trigger when airborne crit conditions are available.
