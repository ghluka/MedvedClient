# Party Games

All-in-one helpers for the Hypixel "Party Games" minigame (/play party_games)

- Animal Slaughter AIO: helps identify and avoid incorrect animals
- Anvil ESP: predicts falling anvil landing positions
- Avalanche ESP: highlights safe wooden slab spots in Avalanche
- Jigsaw Rush Triggerbot: automatically places the correct blocks
- Spider Maze Pathfinder: solves and renders the path to complete the maze

---

### Animal Slaughter AIO

Helps with the Animal Slaughter round by highlighting the correct animals and optionally preventing wrong clicks.

- **Animal ESP**: Draws a box around target animals.
- **Block wrong clicks**: Cancels attack interactions on incorrectly marked animals.
- **Hide wrong mobs**: Temporarily hides mobs marked as “wrong” to reduce clutter.
- **Colors**: Per-species ESP colour settings (cow, pig, chicken).

---

### Anvil ESP

Predicts where a falling anvil will land and renders at the landing block.

- **Anvil ESP color**: Choose the ESP colour.

---

### Avalanche ESP

Renders safe wooden-slab positions in the Avalanche arena.

- **Avalanche ESP color**: Choose the ESP colour.

---

### Jigsaw Rush Triggerbot

Automatically selects the correct block and places when you aim at the puzzle canvas in Jigsaw Rush.

---

### Spider Maze Pathfinder

Renders a path that shows the solution for the Spider Maze minigame.

- **Spider Maze color**: Choose the ESP colour.
