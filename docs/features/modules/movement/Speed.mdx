# Speed <sup><Chip label="Warning" color="warning" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<WarningIcon />}/></sup>

Increases your movement speed

---

### Mode

Choose the speed behavior.

- **ON GROUND**: faster movement while on ground
- **BHOP**: bunny hop movement
- **LOWHOP**: low hop movement style

### Multiplier

Speed multiplier applied to movement.
