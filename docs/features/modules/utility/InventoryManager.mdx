# Inventory Manager

Provides an auto hotbar, auto armour, and inventory cleaner. Calculates the best items and puts them in your specified slots, throws out junk.

---

### Mode

- **INVENTORY**: Only sort when you manually open your inventory
- **ALWAYS**: Automatically open inventory and sort when conditions are met

### Activate On %

(Only in ALWAYS mode) Open inventory and sort when the main inventory is at least this % full. At 0%, it triggers whenever there's any work to do.

### Delay (ms)

Delay between each automatic action (move, throw, equip).

### Auto Hotbar

Automatically moves items into designated hotbar slots based on item type. Set any slot to `-1` to disable it.

#### Hotbar Assignment

- **Sword Slot**: Hotbar position for your best sword (default: 0)
- **Pickaxe Slot**: Hotbar position for your best pickaxe (default: 1)
- **Axe Slot**: Hotbar position for your best axe (default: 2)
- **Bow Slot**: Hotbar position for your best bow (default: 3)
- **Pearl Slot**: Hotbar position for ender pearls (default: 4)
- **Gapple Slot**: Hotbar position for golden apples (default: 5)
- **Block Slot**: Hotbar position for building blocks (default: 6)
- **Food Slot**: Hotbar position for food (default: 7)

### Auto Armor

Enable automatic armor equipping. Finds your best armor pieces and equips them.

#### Prioritize Blast Resistance

When scoring armor, treat Blast Protection as the most valuable enchantment instead of Protection.

### Inventory Cleaner

Automatically throw out unrecognized items that don't fit any hotbar role (not tools, weapons, armor, consumables, or blocks).

#### Trash Extra Gear

Throw out duplicate or worse versions of gear items you already have equipped or assigned to hotbar slots. Useful for preventing inventory clutter when looting.
