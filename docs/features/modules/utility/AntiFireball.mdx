# Anti Fireball

Automatically aims and swings at incoming fireballs to deflect them

---

### Angle limit

Maximum aiming angle within which an incoming fireball is considered.

### Aim speed

How quickly the aim adjusts toward the fireball.

### Stop movement

Stop player movement while targeting an incoming fireball.

### Move on finish

Resume movement after the fireball is deflected.

### Silent aim

Use silent rotation instead of directly changing the player view.
