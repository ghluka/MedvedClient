# Kill Aura <sup><Chip label="Warning" color="warning" size="small" component="a" href="/MedvedClient/guides/anticheat-safety#risk-labels" icon={<WarningIcon />}/></sup>

Automatically attacks enemies in your range.

---

### Mode

Which attack pacing method Kill Aura uses.

- **APS**: attacks per second mode
- **SEQUENTIAL**: attack after an attack cooldown ends

### APS

Attacks per second when in APS mode.

### Rotate

Automatically rotate your server-side pitch and yaw to face the target before attacking.
