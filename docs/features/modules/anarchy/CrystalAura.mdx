# Crystal Aura

Automatically places end crystals on obsidian and detonates them to deal damage to nearby targets.

---

### Target Mode

Determines how the module prioritizes target selection when multiple targets are available.

- **Distance**: Prioritizes the closest targets.
- **Yaw**: Prioritizes targets requiring minimal view angle adjustment.
- **Armor**: Prioritizes targets with weaker armor.
- **Threat**: Prioritizes the most dangerous targets based on their weapon and health level.
- **Health**: Prioritizes targets with the lowest health.

### Range

The maximum distance to check for valid targets.

### Anti-Suicide

Prevents placing or detonating crystals if it would result in fatal self damage.

- **Max Self Damage**: The maximum amount of self-damage allowed per crystal detonation.

### Crystal Optimization

Controls how crystals are evaluated for efficiency.

- **None**: Standard calculation.
- **Rapid fire**: Will quickly attempt sequence looping if efficiency thresholds are met.
- **Predict**: Predicts target movement to calculate theoretical maximum damage ahead.

### Crystal Min Efficiency

The minimum damage output required before placing or detonating a crystal.

### Auto Obsidian

Automatically places obsidian logic blocks downwards to create placement surfaces if necessary.

### Only Players

When enabled, limits targets to only human entities. Disable to target hostile, passive, or allied mobs.
