# Recommended Mods

These are not optimization mods, but rather mods dedicated to improving how your game
feels, looks, or unique quality-of-life features.

### [OpSec](https://modrinth.com/mod/opsec/versions)

Prevents servers from tracing whether you've connected from an alt account
with exploits and fingerprinting.

### [ViaFabricPlus](https://modrinth.com/mod/viafabricplus/versions?l=fabric)

Allows you to connect to servers from older or newer versions, such as 1.8.9 servers.

If you play on a 1.8.9 or older server, try using the
[No Hit Delay](/features/modules/combat/NoHitDelay) for even better hit registration.

### [Zoomify](https://modrinth.com/mod/zoomify/versions?l=fabric)

Lets you zoom your FOV by pressing `C`.
